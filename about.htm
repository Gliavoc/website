About Me

I love cats, watch Star Trek, and I enjoy video games as well. LGBTQA+. Some of my inspirations are Toby Fox, David Bowie, Gene Roddenberry, Stephen Hawking, and Albert Einstein. Teaching people new things is a fun challenging experience, and watching someone else reach that EUREKA moment, is one of the greater joys in life.
